Privacy Policy

App Name: Bhagchal

Last Updated: June 13, 2025

Overview

Bhagchal is a completely offline board game app. This privacy policy explains that we do not collect, store, or share any personal data from our users.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information from users. This includes:

  • No name, email, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage data or analytics
  • No cookies or tracking technologies

Internet Access

Bhagchal is a fully offline application. It does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any servers or third parties.

Third Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any data from children or adults. No personal information is collected at any point.

Data Storage

The app may store game progress locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
